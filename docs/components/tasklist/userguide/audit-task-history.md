Audit [task history](../../user-operations-audit-log/overview.md) in Camunda 8 Tasklist.

## Prerequisites

To follow the steps in this guide, you must be [authorized to view task history records](../../user-operations-audit-log/overview/access-control.md).

## Audit task history

1. On the **Tasks** page, click a task to audit.
2. In the tasks detail view, click the **History** tab.
3. To sort the log, click a column header.
4. To see the details of a particular operation, click the info icon at the end of the row.
