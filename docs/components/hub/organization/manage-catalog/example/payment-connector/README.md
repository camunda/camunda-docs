## Payment Connector

Integrates with the company payment gateway to handle payment operations within your business processes.

### Capabilities

- Initiate payments
- Check payment status
- Process refunds

### Usage

Apply this template to a **Service Task** in your BPMN diagram. Configure the payment operation and amount in the properties panel.

The underlying job worker must be deployed to your Orchestration Cluster by your Center of Excellence.
